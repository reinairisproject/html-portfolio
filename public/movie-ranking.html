Reina's personal favourite movies so far

My top 3 movies

1. A Star is Born

It's probably the only movie that I sobbed HARD watching.
Very memorable.

2. Kimi no Na Wa

To be honest I don't watch movies that often but I liked how this movie kind of reminded me of Steins:Gate with the time travelling aspect.
Visually stunning.

3. Incidious series

I generally quite enjoy horror movies. This one actually had a well thought out storyline in my opinion.
I liked the fact it didn't just rely on gore or jumpscares.
